Our trainer uses mathematically optimal blackjack strategy to provide instant feedback on every decision you make. You'll learn when to hit, stand, double down, or split based on your cards and the dealer's up-card.

Absolutely! The trainer is designed for all skill levels, from complete beginners to advanced players. You'll start with the basics and gradually learn more complex strategies at your own pace.

Can I practice card counting with this trainer?

Absolutely! Our trainer includes dedicated card counting modules that teach you various systems like Hi-Lo, KO (Knock-Out), and Omega II. You can practice counting in realistic scenarios with multiple decks and varying game speeds to build your skills gradually.
